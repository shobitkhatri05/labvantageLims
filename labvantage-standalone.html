Why LabVantage?

Enterprise LIMS Built for Complex, High-Throughput Labs

Scalable, secure, and fully integrated—designed to power compliant operations across every site.

Disconnected systems and spreadsheets make samples and progress hard to track. LabVantage delivers a single source of truth with real-time visibility across workflows—and securely integrates instruments, enterprise systems, and external partners to break down silos and accelerate innovation across your network.

  • Single source of truth across samples and workflows
  • Real-time visibility into laboratory performance
  • Continuous business-process and IT alignment at lab pace
  • Faster rollout of innovations across the network
  • Seamless instrument and enterprise integration

Manual handling and evolving regulations introduce risk and rework. Standardised workflows, automated data capture, and complete electronic audit trails deliver reliable, ALCOA+ aligned data—keeping you inspection-ready and building a strong foundation for analytics and AI across operations.

  • ALCOA+ aligned data integrity
  • Standardised workflows and automated capture
  • Controlled processes with full traceability
  • Electronic records and complete audit trails
  • Strong foundation for analytics and AI

Laboratory needs evolve over time—new sites, modalities, and integrations come fast. LabVantage offers a modular, scalable platform that grows with your requirements, adding capabilities incrementally without forcing system replacement, so investments compound rather than reset as your organisation expands.

  • Modular architecture, add capabilities incrementally
  • Multi-site and multi-modality ready
  • From single lab to global network
  • No rip-and-replace—protects existing investments

Traditional systems are costly to maintain and difficult to roll out globally. LabVantage runs in any standard browser with no local install, consolidates point tools through broad functional coverage, and can be deployed on-premises or in the cloud to support data residency and IT governance.

  • Zero-install, runs in any standard browser
  • On-premises or cloud deployment flexibility
  • Broad functional coverage replaces multiple point tools
  • Supports global data residency requirements
  • Lower TCO and reduced IT governance burden
